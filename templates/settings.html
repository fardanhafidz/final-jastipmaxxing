About JASTIPMAXXING

A route optimization system using Genetic Algorithms to solve the Precedence-Constrained Traveling Salesman Problem (PC-TSP).

How it works

  • Basecamp — starting & ending point
  • Pickup — shops to collect orders
  • Delivery — destinations for each pickup

The algorithm ensures every pickup happens before its deliveries, and finds the shortest route visiting all points via real roads (OSRM).

GA Operators

  • Selection — top 25% breed next generation
  • Crossover — Order Crossover (OX) preserves gene order
  • Mutation — swap mutation for diversity
  • Elitism — best solutions survive unchanged
